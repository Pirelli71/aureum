### Appendix D: Glossary of Terms

**DeFi (Decentralized Finance):** Financial applications built on blockchain networks, operating without traditional intermediaries like banks.

**LP (Liquidity Provider):** Users who deposit token pairs into liquidity pools to enable trading, earning fees in return.

**IL (Impermanent Loss):** Temporary loss experienced by LPs when token price ratios change compared to simply holding the assets.

**TVL (Total Value Locked):** Total value of assets deposited in DeFi protocols, measured in USD.

**APY (Annual Percentage Yield):** Annualized return on investment including compound interest.

**Yield Farming:** Strategy of moving capital between DeFi protocols to maximize returns.

**Pool:** Smart contract holding liquidity for decentralized exchanges or lending protocols.

**Protocol:** DeFi application with specific functionality (e.g., Uniswap for trading, Aave for lending).

**Multi-chain:** Operating across multiple blockchain networks (Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, etc.).

**RPC (Remote Procedure Call):** API endpoint for reading blockchain data.

**TAM/SAM/SOM:** Market sizing framework (Total Addressable Market / Serviceable Addressable Market / Serviceable Obtainable Market).

**LTV (Lifetime Value):** Total revenue expected from a customer over their lifetime.

**CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Cost to acquire one paying customer.

**MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue):** Predictable monthly subscription revenue.

**ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue):** MRR × 12, standard SaaS metric.

**Churn Rate:** % of customers who cancel subscriptions per period.

**CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate):** Year-over-year growth rate over multiple years.

**Freemium:** Business model offering free basic tier and paid premium tiers.

**Gross Margin:** (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue, profitability metric.

**EBITDA:** Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization.
